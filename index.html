End-to-End Encryption

Photos and Videos are stored in encrypted format. They are also transmitted in encrypted format between our cloud and user devices. Media files shared between YourPhotos users are also transmitted in encrypted form within our secure cloud. Because data is encrypted at all steps, the risk of data leaks is largely eliminated.

Zero Access to User Data

Zero access architecture means that your data is encrypted in a way that makes it inaccessible to us. Data is encrypted on the client side using an encryption key that we do not have access to. This means we don't have the technical ability to decrypt your photos or videos, and as a result, we are unable to hand your data over to third parties. If you forget your key, we cannot recover your data.

Open Source

YourPhotos is open source so security experts can verify the code that protects your data. Our Android app is Google-free making YourPhotos the best open source encrypted photo vault.

Back up

You can back up your newly taken photos or videos in background using apps for iOS and Android. Free up space by removing backed up files.
